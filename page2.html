Welcome

The Quality Training Academy is a complete set of courses from keytoquality.com, designed to provide the quality training you need, at the appropriate level and pace thats right for you.

All of the modules in our courses come complete with a transcript, a copy of the slide deck, and supporting material like worksheets and templates.

We are proud of our high production quality which breaks the mold from traditional training in this area. On our youtube channel you can find our introductory modules for free.

Continuing Professional Development

It is important for a business to ensure all staff keep their skills and knowledge up to date. Having a subscription to the Quality Training Academy means sustaining knowledge in the team, and the online format allows managers to define tailored and planned training for each individual's personal development. Companies don't need to lose people, time or resources to arrange and carry out the training you need, instead let the training work around your schedule.

Company Onboarding Program

Specialized quality professionals are becoming more sought after in modern business. An option for employers is to onboard trained professionals in their field of study into a quality based role. The Quality Training Academy from K2Q can be used and branded as your in house training program to bring new and existing employees who may not have a complete background in quality up to speed.

On site training and support

All of our training can be delivered as full in house training courses. We can also work with you to understand your individual requirements to deliver a tailored training program. We offer 1 days worth of free on site training each year with Business subscribers to the QTA (terms and conditions apply).



Global Training

Many businesses work with overseas manufacturers. Our training course content is delivered in multiple languages to enable a consistent level understanding between global teams, avoiding language barriers.
