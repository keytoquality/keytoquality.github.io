Introducing K2Q Framework

What is it?

K2Q is a cloud based quality data management system that is customer focused to aid in failure reporting, corrective action and continuous improvement activities.

How does it work?

K2Q integrates into your current system and processes, you choose the modules you want, and the system grows with your business. Our modules are based on industry standard practices and help you to correctly integrate these methods into your workflow, giving you the data insight you need without complex programming or spreadsheets.

K2Q Framework Modules

K2FRACAS

Based on the industry standard "Failure Reporting and Corrective Action System" ideaology, and the core of the K2Q Framework. Includes tools for capturing incidents and highlighting failure modes consistently across the entire product or process life cycle. Automatically generate key metrics based on the data, giving you powerful insights and presenting you with the information so you can spend less time collating information and more time taking actions. Integrates seamlessly with other modules for added functionailty such as automatic Failure Mode categorisation based on information from an FMEA.

This module also comes with 2 industry standard problem solving methods (8D and DMAIC) each with built in workflows that take you through the process of problem solving and implementing corrective actions via efficient and user friendly interfaces. This ensures a consistent approach to all quality issues and gives you a closed loop system where lessons learnt can be effectively fedback into design or production processes. This functionality can be expanded with our Root Causes Analysis add on module.

K2FMEA

A structured approach to implementing and guiding you through FMEA's 

K2KPI

Adds a realtime KPI dashboard to any module, comes with a selection of ready to go KPI's and measures, with the ability to create your own.

K2OEE

Adds functionality to analyse repariable system failures for achieving maximum operational uptime of resources and equipment, aswell as providing a shift based reporting system that can be implemented in factories. Connect to a wide variety of equipment and sensors for downtime monitoring purposes using our K2API.

K2RCA

A comprehensive suit of Root Cause Analysis tools used to complement other modules or use by itself.

K2REL

Track the reliability of collected data and perform predictive analysis. Perfect complement to our K2FRACAS and K2OEE modules.
