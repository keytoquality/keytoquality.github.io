Introduction to problem solving is a primer module for our complete problem solving course, which includes seperate modules that go into the specific aspects and methods of problem solving in more detail. At the end of the module you will have an awareness of what problem solving is, why and where it is carried out, and what is required to conduct it.

This introductory module can be used as a tool on its own for teams and professionals to gain a common level of understanding regarding terminology and basic concepts and processes. This is important for cross functional teams where participants in the problem solving process may not necessarily have a background in quality. This module provides the key information for stakeholder buy in at the appropriate level and pace that's right for you.
