K2Q Framework

What does it do

The K2Q Framework takes advantage of our powerful workflow engine. Our software provides a complete set of structured, guided workflows which present complex processes in a way that's easier to implement and manage, with a level of customization to suit your preferences.

A structured and consistent approach is essential to maintaining high quality standards. However, we have also integrated fluid conversations into our workflows, so that creativity is not stifled by boundaries during collaborative excercises like problem solving and FMEA.
