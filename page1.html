K2Q Framework

What is it?

K2Q is a quality management system that is data focused. The foundation of this system is a robust Failure Reporting and Corrective Action platform.

How does it work?

K2Q integrates into your current system and processes, you choose the modules you want, we help you configure them based on your requirement. Additional modules can be seamlessly added at a later date with minimal setup. No complex coding and friendly user interfaces.
